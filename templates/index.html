About Our Project

The Digital Waste Tracker is an innovative solution developed to simplify and modernize waste management across Sydney. By combining technology, sustainability, and data insights, our platform empowers individuals, organizations, and councils to track, manage, and report waste efficiently. We believe that smart monitoring and digital transparency can help create a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.
