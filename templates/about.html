About the Digital Waste Tracking System

The Digital Waste Tracking System is an innovative platform designed to revolutionize waste management through technology and data insights. Our goal is to enable cities, recycling centers, and organizations to efficiently track, manage, and analyze waste movement in real time. By integrating smart monitoring tools and automated reporting, the system helps reduce manual errors, enhance sustainability, and support eco-friendly decision-making.
