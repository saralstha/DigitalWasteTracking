About Digital Waste Tracking

TEST UPDATE WORKING

A smart solution designed to improve waste management and promote sustainability through digital reporting, tracking, and analytics.

Our Vision

The Digital Waste Tracking System aims to transform how waste is managed in modern cities. By leveraging technology, we enable individuals and organizations to report, monitor, and track waste efficiently. Our goal is to build a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future.

Key Features

Live Tracking

Track reported waste locations in real-time using interactive maps.

Easy Reporting

Users can quickly report waste with location, type, and image.

User Accounts

Secure login system with profile management and tracking history.

Data Insights

Analyze waste patterns for better environmental decisions.

Our Impact

By enabling digital waste tracking, this system helps reduce illegal dumping, improve recycling efficiency, and support smart city initiatives. It empowers communities to actively participate in environmental protection.
